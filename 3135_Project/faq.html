Working as a Niner Guide is a volunteer on campus opportunity. Unfortunately only srudent that apply and get selected for leadership positions can get paid biweekly

To get to Gold Status you must pick up one additonal tour each week apart from your own tour shift.

As a regular Niner Guide you will need to work around 2 hours each week during your tour time to give tour and we have biweekly meetings that are a hour long. Timings vary for trainees and for Leadership positions.

Each Niner Guide is given two polos, a green one and a black one. These are the only shirts that you may wear during your tours. There is also a specific dress code that Guides must follow on tours and in Office.

You can apply to this position by using the link on this website the positions open each semster but the number of candidates selected each semester vary. You will need to fill out a google form that asks few vasic queation about you and why you are interested in the posiiton. Once you fill that out your application will be viewed and we will let you know your results.
