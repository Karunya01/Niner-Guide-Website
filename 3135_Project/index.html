About Us

  Niner Guides are a team of current UNC Charlotte students who are passionate about sharing our vibrant campus and diverse
community with you. From academics and research to student life and athletics, we are here to guide you through your first visit to
Charlotte, and show you the endless opportunities we have.


What We Do

  We provide out prospective students and their families with comprehensive tours of our campus. Each tailored to students that are looking forward to join the family.


Our Commitment

  We are commited to welcoming and creating an inclusive environment for our visitors and prospective students, by answering your questions, sharing our experinces, and helping you picture yourself at our school. Our goal is to make sure you feel connected to our community and decide to join us!
